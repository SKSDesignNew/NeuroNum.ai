Brand Identity System

NeuroNum.ai

Where Neurons Meet Numbers — AI-Powered Science & Finance for High School

Concept 1 — Brain-Chart Fusion (Primary)

Left half: neuron network with pulsing synapses (science). Right half: ascending bar chart with trend line (finance). Connected by an animated signal bridge. The full story in one circular mark.

Concept 2 — Double Helix (Light Mode)

A DNA double helix where the left strand is teal (science) and right strand is gold (finance), connected by base-pair bridges. Symbolizes the intertwined nature of both disciplines. Elegant on light backgrounds.

Concept 3 — Split Brain

A brain split down the center — left hemisphere shows neural folds with pulsing synapses (science), right hemisphere shows ascending bar charts and a $ symbol (finance). United by a gradient spine. The most iconic, most recognizable mark.

Concept 4 — Molecule Coin (Light)

A molecular hexagon where science atoms (C, H, O in teal) and finance symbols ($, %, π in gold) sit at each vertex, with AI at the nucleus. The outer ring echoes a coin. Science and finance are literally bonded together.
